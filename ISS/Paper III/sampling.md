- Equality condition

# Part 7: Important Derivations to Prepare

## Must-Know Derivations (100% Important)

### Derivation 1: Neyman Allocation

$$\phi = \sum_{i=1}^{k} \frac{W_i^2 S_i^2}{n_i} + \lambda\left(n - \sum_{i=1}^{k} n_i\right)$$

$$\frac{\partial \phi}{\partial n_i} = -\frac{W_i^2 S_i^2}{n_i^2} - \lambda = 0$$

$$n_i = \frac{W_i S_i}{\sqrt{-\lambda}}$$

Using constraint:

$$\boxed{n_i = n \cdot \frac{W_i S_i}{\sum_{j=1}^{k} W_j S_j}}$$

---

### Derivation 2: Variance Comparison

**SRS Variance:**
$$Var(\bar{y}_{SRS}) = \frac{1-f}{n}S^2$$

**Proportional Allocation Variance:**
$$Var(\bar{y}_{prop}) = \frac{1-f}{n}\sum W_i S_i^2$$

**Difference:**
$$Var(\bar{y}_{SRS}) - Var(\bar{y}_{prop}) = \frac{1-f}{n}\left[S^2 - \sum W_i S_i^2\right]$$

$$= \frac{1-f}{n}\sum W_i(\bar{Y}_i - \bar{Y})^2 \geq 0$$

$$\boxed{Var(\bar{y}_{prop}) \leq Var(\bar{y}_{SRS})}$$

---

### Derivation 3: Ratio Estimator Variance

Let $\bar{y}_R = \frac{\bar{y}}{\bar{x}}\bar{X}$

Using Taylor expansion:

$$\bar{y}_R \approx \bar{Y} + (\bar{y} - \bar{Y}) - R(\bar{x} - \bar{X})$$

$$Var(\bar{y}_R) \approx Var(\bar{y} - R\bar{x})$$

$$= Var(\bar{y}) + R^2 Var(\bar{x}) - 2R \cdot Cov(\bar{y}, \bar{x})$$

$$\boxed{Var(\bar{y}_R) \approx \frac{1-f}{n}(S_y^2 + R^2 S_x^2 - 2RS_{xy})}$$

---

### Derivation 4: Efficiency Condition for Ratio Estimator

For ratio estimator to be better than SRS:

$$Var(\bar{y}_R) < Var(\bar{y}_{SRS})$$

$$S_y^2 + R^2 S_x^2 - 2RS_{xy} < S_y^2$$

$$R^2 S_x^2 < 2RS_{xy}$$

$$R S_x^2 < 2S_{xy}$$

$$\frac{\bar{Y}}{\bar{X}} S_x^2 < 2\rho S_x S_y$$

$$\frac{\bar{Y} S_x}{\bar{X} S_y} < 2\rho$$

$$\frac{C_x}{C_y} < 2\rho$$

$$\boxed{\rho > \frac{C_x}{2C_y}}$$

# Part 8: Important Formulas Quick Reference

## SRS Formulas

| Estimator | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| Mean | $\bar{y} = \frac{1}{n}\sum y_i$ |
| Variance of Mean | $Var(\bar{y}) = \frac{1-f}{n}S^2$ |
| Estimated Variance | $\hat{V}(\bar{y}) = \frac{1-f}{n}s^2$ |
| Population Total | $\hat{Y} = N\bar{y}$ |

---

## Stratified Sampling Formulas

| Allocation | $n_i$ Formula | Variance |
|------------|---------------|----------|
| Proportional | $n_i = nW_i$ | $\frac{1-f}{n}\sum W_i S_i^2$ |
| Neyman | $n_i = n\frac{W_i S_i}{\sum W_j S_j}$ | $\frac{1}{n}\left(\sum W_i S_i\right)^2$ |
| Cost Optimum | $n_i \propto \frac{W_i S_i}{\sqrt{C_i}}$ | Minimum for given cost |

---

## Ratio and Product Estimation

| Estimator | Formula | Efficiency Condition |
|-----------|---------|----------------------|
| Ratio | $\bar{y}_R = \frac{\bar{y}}{\bar{x}}\bar{X}$ | $\rho > \frac{C_x}{2C_y}$ |
| Product | $\bar{y}_P = \bar{y} \cdot \frac{\bar{x}}{\bar{X}}$ | $\rho < -\frac{C_x}{2C_y}$ |
| Regression | $\bar{y}_{reg} = \bar{y} + b(\bar{X} - \bar{x})$ | Always ≥ Ratio |

---

## Systematic Sampling

| Aspect | Formula/Result |
|--------|----------------|
| Sample Mean | $\bar{y}_{sys} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n} y_{i+jk}$ |
| Variance | Depends on population structure |
| Better than SRS when | Intra-class correlation is negative |
| Worse than SRS when | Periodic patterns exist |

---

## Cluster Sampling

| Aspect | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| Estimator | $\bar{y}_c = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} M_i \bar{y}_i}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} M_i}$ |
| Design Effect | $D_{eff} = 1 + (M-1)\rho$ |
| Efficient when | $\rho$ is small or negative |
