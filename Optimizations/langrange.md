# Problem with Equality Constraint and Inequality Constraint

## Introduction


Consider the constrained optimization problem:

Minimize $f(x)$ \
subject to $h(x)=0$ \
$g(x)\le 0$ 

where h: $R^n \rightarrow R^m $ \
g: $R^n \rightarrow R^p $ 

Any point satisfying the constraint is feasible point
